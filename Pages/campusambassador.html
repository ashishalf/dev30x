Campus Ambassador

InternDev provides a variety of internship opportunities for 1st and 2nd year students who are interested in pursuing a career in the tech industry. The website offers internships in various fields such as web development, android development, python development, java development, graphic designing, and campus ambassador.

InternDev not only provides internship opportunities, but it also gives interns a chance to work on real-world projects and complete tasks within a specific time period. This hands-on experience helps interns to develop their skills and gain practical knowledge of the industry.

Internship Details:

The Campus Ambassador Program at InternDEV is designed to empower students who are passionate about networking, career development, and helping others succeed. As a Campus Ambassador, you will represent InternDEV on your campus, foster relationships, and promote the platform to fellow students. You will act as a liaison between your campus and our platform, while gaining insights into marketing, networking, and community engagement.

Join our Campus Ambassador Program at InternDEV and become a vital part of our mission to connect students with incredible internship opportunities. As a Campus Ambassador, you'll not only enhance your own skills but also contribute to the professional growth of your fellow students.

Requirements:

  • • Strong verbal and written communication skills are essential for effectively promoting the program.
  • • The ability to build and maintain relationships with various individuals, including students.
  • • Basic marketing knowledge to create engaging content, utilize social media effectively, and spread awareness about the internship platform..
  • • Generating innovative ideas for events, content, and engagement strategies.

Summary:

  • • As a Campus Ambassador, you'll enhance your own skills and contribute to the professional growth of your fellow students..
  • • Completion of assigned tasks is a requirement of the internship.
  • • The internship duration is 1 month.
  • • Internship completion certificate.
