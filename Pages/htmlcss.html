HTML/CSS Challenge

Welcome to the HTML/CSS Challenge as part of the 30DaysOfCoding initiative! This challenge is designed to help you master the foundational skills of web development by focusing on HTML (HyperText Markup Language) and CSS (Cascading Style Sheets). Over the next 30 days, you'll embark on a journey to build visually stunning, responsive websites from scratch.

This challenge is perfect for beginners looking to build a solid foundation in web development or for anyone who wants to refresh their skills. By the end of the 30 days, you'll have a portfolio of completed projects that showcase your ability to design and build modern websites.

Get ready to code, create, and elevate your web development skills with the HTML/CSS Challenge!

How to Start:

  • 1. Click the "Start Now" Button: Begin your journey by clicking the "Start Now" button on this page.
  • 2. Download the PDF: A PDF will open up containing all the tasks. Download this PDF to keep track of your daily challenges.
  • 3. Complete the Tasks in 30 Days: Follow the tasks outlined in the PDF. Aim to complete each one within the 30-day timeframe to build and enhance your HTML/CSS skills.
  • 4. Upload Your Code to GitHub: Create a GitHub repository and upload your code day by day. This will help you track your progress and showcase your work.

What You'll Learn:

  • • HTML Basics: Start with the essentials—learn to structure your content using HTML tags, elements, and attributes.
  • • CSS Fundamentals: Discover how to style your web pages with CSS, including working with colors, fonts, layouts, and more.
  • • Responsive Design: Learn techniques to make your websites look great on any device, from desktops to smartphones.
  • • Advanced Techniques: Delve into more complex concepts like Flexbox, Grid, and CSS animations to create dynamic and engaging user experiences.
