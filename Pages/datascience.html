Data Science

InternDEV provides a variety of skill program opportunities for 1st and 2nd year students who are interested in pursuing a career in the tech industry. InternDEV offers skill programs in various fields such as Web Development, Android Development, Python, Java, UI/UX, and Data Science.

InternDEV not only provides skill program opportunities but it also gives students a chance to work on real-world projects and complete tasks within a specific time period. This hands-on experience helps students to develop their skills and gain practical knowledge of the industry.

Program Details:

Our data science skill program offers hands-on experience in leveraging data to derive valuable insights and solve real-world problems. Responsibilities include data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, model development, and effective communication of findings. This skill program provides a dynamic learning environment for individuals passionate about data analytics, machine learning, and statistical analysis.

Join us to gain practical skills, enhance your problem-solving abilities, and make a meaningful impact in the field of data science.

Requirements:

  • • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python.
  • • Understanding of statistical concepts and techniques is crucial for exploratory data analysis, hypothesis testing, and building predictive models.
  • • Python libraries for data science include NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib,
  • • Ability to clean and preprocess raw data effectively using tools like Pandas or scikit-learn.

Summary:

  • • Gain valuable hands-on experience in Data Science and Machine Learning.
  • • Completion of assigned tasks is a requirement of the skill program.
  • • The skill program duration is 1 month.
  • • Skill program completion certificate.
